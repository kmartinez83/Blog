Typical Dia de Los Muertos Dishes

Pan De Muertos

Ingredients:

  • 4 ounces butter
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3 teaspons whole aniseed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 cups of flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoon orange zest
  • 2 (1/4 ounce) packets instant dry yeast

Step 1: Gather the ingredients.
Step 2: In the bowl of a stand mixer, combine butter, sugar, aniseed, salt, and 1/2 cup of the flour.
Step 3: Use the dough hook to mix the ingredients until they begin to come together.
Step 4: In a separate small bowl, whisk together the eggs, water, and orange zest.
Step 5: Add this to the stand mixer bowl, along with another 1/2 cup of the flour. Mix until combined.
Step 6: Add the yeast and another 1/2 cup of flour, mixing to combine.
Step 7:Add the remaining flour 1 cup at a time, mixing between additions, until a dough forms.
Step 8:Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead it for 1 minute.
Step 9: Cover it with a clean, damp dishcloth and let rise in a warm area for 1 hour and 30 minutes.
Step 10: Separate about 1/4 of the dough and use it to make bone shapes to drape across the loaf.
Step 11:Shape the rest of the dough into a flat-bottomed semi-sphere. Position the bone shapes on the top of the loaf and press gently so they adhere. Let the dough rise for an additional hour.
Step 12: Bake the loaf in a 350 F oven for about 40 minutes (30 minutes for smaller loaves).
Step 13:Cool and glaze, if desired, before serving.
Step 14: Cut pan de muerto into large wedges for eating by hand. Serve it with Mexican hot chocolate

Enjoy!
